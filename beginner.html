Surface 180

The Surface 180 is a fundamental trick where you rotate 180 degrees while riding on the water's surface. This maneuver serves as the foundation for many advanced rotational tricks and helps you get comfortable with changing directions while riding. It's excellent for developing balance and cable control. Keep your eyes focused on a point during the rotation and remember to keep your arms steady.

Surface 360

Building on the Surface 180, the Surface 360 involves a full rotation on the water's surface. This trick helps develop your balance and control while spinning, preparing you for aerial rotations. The key is to maintain your gaze fixed on a point during the rotation to maintain orientation. Start slow and progressively increase your rotation speed as you get more comfortable with the movement.

Front Lip

The Front Lip is a basic obstacle trick where you slide along a rail or box with the front of your board. It's one of the first tricks you'll learn on obstacles and is crucial for developing confidence and control on rails. Keep your weight centered and your eyes focused on the obstacle's exit. Start with small, low rails and gradually progress to larger features as your confidence builds. Remember to keep your knees slightly bent to absorb any bumps.
